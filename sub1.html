IN ABANDONED GOODS GET INSPIRED.

FREITAG was created by Marcus Freitag and Daniel Freitag Brothers in Zurich, Switzerland. The two brothers, who were designers, went on bicycles, and on rainy days, we needed a sturdy bag that did not wet the sketch paper. Then a truck goes in front of them and the inspired Marcus goes to a nearby waste truck and returns with an abandoned truck lid, a car seat belt, and an abandoned bicycle wheel. After washing the truck cloth in the bathroom, she starts working with her mother's sewing machine to create the first 'messenger bag'. It was the moment when things that would have been worn out were reborn as the only bag in the world through the Brotherhood.

FREITAG

STRIKING MATERIAL.

"Honestly, it was a coincidence that we first used 'recycled products'. When I first made my bag, my house was attached to the highway. Originally, I was going to make a waterproof bag, but I wondered what a sturdy fabric would be like when it was waterproof, and then I looked at the truck running on the highway and thought, 'I can do that.' I could contact fabric suppliers, but I was more desirous to find new materials as a designer. I asked the shipping company if I could get thousands of trucks to dispose of, and I made my first bag with it. The bag was perfect for the functionality we thought it would be. It's hard and waterproof. From the beginning we will only use 'recyclables'. I did not think the same thing. " When I looked for functionality, it was a recycled material that stood out by chance. "

MARKUS FREITAG
